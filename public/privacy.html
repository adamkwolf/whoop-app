What Data We Collect

When you connect your WHOOP account, we access:

  • Recovery scores and heart rate variability data
  • Daily strain and activity metrics
  • Sleep performance and sleep stage information
  • Workout data including strain and heart rate
  • Basic profile information (name, email)
  • Body measurements (height, weight, max heart rate)

How We Use Your Data

We use your WHOOP data to:

  • Display your fitness metrics in a personalized dashboard
  • Provide insights into your health and fitness trends
  • Enable you to track your progress over time

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is:

  • Stored securely using industry-standard encryption
  • Kept only as long as necessary to provide our services
  • Never shared with advertisers or data brokers
  • Protected by WHOOP's OAuth 2.0 security protocols

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access to your WHOOP data at any time
  • Request deletion of your stored data
  • Access and review the data we have collected
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns
